Your Projects

Make water from fuel

Break down the hydrazine to strip out hydrogen, then light it up with Hab oxygen to get water vapor. Catch the vapor, condense it, and try not to blow myself sky-high in the process.

Growing taters

Plant the potatoes in Martian soil mixed with my own fertilizer, then keep them alive with water I make and the Hab’s atmosphere. If they don’t die, I won’t either.

Using Pathfinder to talk to NASA

Pathfinder’s still out there—if I can dig it up and power it, maybe I can hack its systems to talk to Earth. No guarantees it’ll work after decades in the dirt, but it’s my only shot at saying “Hey, still alive over here.”

Keeping myself warm in Rover

The rover freezes at night, so I’ll haul in the RTG and let its steady radiation heat do the work. Risky to ride around with a chunk of nuclear material, but freezing to death sounds worse.

Mars Disco

Hook up the Hab’s spare lights to blink in sequence, blast some 70s music from my laptop, and call it the first nightclub on Mars. It’s pointless, but if I’m gonna be the only guy here, I might as well be the DJ too.
